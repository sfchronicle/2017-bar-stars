Bar Stars 2017

The 2017 class of Bar Stars reflects the fact that great cocktails are today being made in every genre of establishment. These five bartenders work in Italian restaurants (Beretta; 54 Mint), in 100-year-old sailor hangouts (the Sea Star), in hotels (Dirty Habit). Even in a bar (Pagan Idol) where faux volcano periodically erupts and scorpion bowls are served aflame — yes, a bar that’s that kitschy — can churn out world-class cocktails.
